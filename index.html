About Us

TEAM ORION

We, TEAM ORION is a group of six people who wants to pursue more on eclipses. Our motive is to explain the mechanics of eclipses and its related aspects to young learners. We are interested to study more about this beautiful domain of science and we would like to share our works and findings before others. The major speciality of our team is the urge to learn and our team spirit. We are excited to share our work before other aspiring people who also want to pursue more on this domain
